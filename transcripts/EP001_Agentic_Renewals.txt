EP001: Agentic Renewals
=======================

In this episode of the Chief Customer Officer Podcast, Jay Nathan and Jeff Breunsbach discuss the evolving landscape of customer success, particularly focusing on the impact of AI. They explore the importance of building a robust renewal function, identifying key information for renewals, and the necessity of mapping out processes. 

The conversation delves into the development of granular AI agents to enhance customer interactions and the balance between workflow and AI capabilities. They also emphasize the significance of understanding customer business contexts and choosing the right tools for effective AI implementation. 

The episode concludes with thoughts on standardizing AI use across teams and the future directions of AI in customer success.
